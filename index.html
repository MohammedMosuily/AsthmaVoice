AsthmaVoice Study

Conducted at the University of Southampton, exploring how speech can predict asthma exacerbations.

About the Study

AsthmaVoice is a research project led by PhD student Mohammed Mosuily, under the supervision of Dr. Jagmohan Chauhan. The study investigates the potential of speech to predict asthma symptoms and improve early intervention.

For more details about the research, kindly read the participant information sheet.
